Project Name : El-Sir

Project Description :

El-Sir is an Enterprise Management system designed for small and medium Enterprises (SMES). It is uniquely designed
with features specific for the operations and way of business for SMES. It will be used to record daily sales 
transactions, accounting reports. It also provides the customers module where customers can interact with the 
business and offers the business owner a direct link to customers for sales and marketing purposes.
